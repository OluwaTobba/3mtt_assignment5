MI WATCHES FAQ(s)

1. What sets MI Watches apart from other wristwatch brands?

MI Watches stands out for its fusion of timeless elegance and cutting-edge design. Each timepiece is meticulously crafted, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation.

2. Are MI Watches water-resistant?

Yes, MI Watches are designed to be water-resistant. However, the level of resistance may vary across models, so it's recommended to check the specific details for each watch.

3. Do MI Watches come with a warranty?

Yes, MI Watches come with a limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Please refer to the warranty information provided with your purchase for details on coverage and duration.

4. Can I replace the watch strap or bracelet?

MI Watches offer a range of interchangeable straps and bracelets for personalization. Check our accessories section for options that complement your style.

5. Can I engrave a personalized message on my MI Watch?

Yes, we offer engraving services for select models. Explore the personalization options during the checkout process on our website.

6. How do I care for and maintain my MI Watch?

To ensure longevity, avoid exposing your watch to extreme temperatures, chemicals, or magnetic fields. Regularly clean the watch with a soft, dry cloth and have it serviced by authorized professionals periodically.
